üìà Understanding Performance Metrics

Key Metrics Explained:

  • Accuracy: Overall correctness (can mislead with imbalanced data)
  • ROC AUC: Discrimination ability (0.5‚Äì1.0, higher better)
  • Precision: Accuracy of positive predictions (minimize false positives)
  • Recall: Coverage of actual positives (minimize false negatives)
  • F1-Score: Harmonic mean of Precision & Recall (balanced metric)
  • üè• In Healthcare: Recall > Accuracy (don't miss at-risk patients!)

üí° Medical Context: Missing a patient who will have a long stay is more costly than a false positive. Therefore, Recall and ROC AUC are prioritized over raw Accuracy.

‚ö†Ô∏è Caveats & Limitations

1Ô∏è‚É£ Dataset Size & Representativeness

10k patient records may not capture all hospital types, demographics, or regional variations. Results may not generalize to all healthcare settings or populations

2Ô∏è‚É£ Feature Limitations & Data Quality

3Ô∏è‚É£ Model Interpretability Trade-off
